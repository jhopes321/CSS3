Barcelona

Barcelona is the cosmopolitan capital of Spain's Catalonia region. It is Spain's Second most populated city, with 1.6 million people.It was founded as a roman city in the middle ages.

It is one of the world's leading tourist,economic,trade fair and coultural centers.Barcelona has many museums located in different areas, they also have sixty-eight municipal parks.
